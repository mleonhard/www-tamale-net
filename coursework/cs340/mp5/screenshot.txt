King's Corner commands:
 Q                 - Quit: quit the program
 H                 - Help: show this information
 A                 - About: tell about the programer and program
 D                 - Draw: draw a card and end your turn
 L <Card> <Pile>   - Lay: lay a card on a pile
 M <Pile1> <Pile2> - Move: move Pile1 on top of Pile2

The game consists of several rounds. A round is played until a player has layed
down all cards in the hand.  The player still holding cards receives penalty
points: 10 point for each King and 1 point for every other card.  Rounds are
played until one player receives 25 or more penalty points.  The winner of the
game is the player with the fewest points.  Cards may be stacked in piles, in
descending rank and alternating color.  Piles 1-4 may begin with any card.
Piles 5-8 may begin only with Kings.  For detailed rules, please visit:
http://www.pagat.com/domino/kingscorners.html
